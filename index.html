What changed.

Coleaseum is becoming Colet. The mission — making rentals less painful — hasn't changed, but the product is being rebuilt from the ground up with a sharper focus.

It started at the University of Waterloo: two friends frustrated by the chaos of finding sublets and leases. Two years of building Coleaseum taught us what users actually need, and we're folding those lessons into a cleaner platform under a name that's faster to type and easier to remember.

The new home is colet.ai · colet.rent and colet.io point the same way. This page sticks around as a marker of where we started.

  
  

Features.

Matchmaking - Our proprietary AI-driven roommate-tenant matchmaking algorithm simplifies the task of moving to a new city.

Security - Profile verifications and reviews are used to ensure a safer and more secure environment.

Bidding - Coleaseum's Unique Bidding Marketplace allows users to bid for rent to guarantee fair market price.

Community - Our platform aims to build lifelong relationships starting from university, growing with our users over time.
